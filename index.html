About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio.

Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Software at Northeastern University.

In this portfolio, I will delve deep into my experiences, projects, skills, and hobbies, providing you with a comprehensive understanding of who I am as both a developer and a person.

BattleShip Online

For the culminating project in my Object-Oriented Design course, our professor challenged us to develop a battleship application. This engaging app was designed for student play, incorporating interactions with the university server. Beyond the creation of a fully functional game, our task extended to implementing an algorithm that would function as a player, interacting with algorithms crafted by fellow students to determine their effectiveness. Over the course of a collaborative week, my team and I successfully secured a position within the top 10 among 300 students in the class. The project offered valuable hands-on experience in the application of MVC principles, effective collaboration via GitHub, navigating teamwork dynamics, and delivering high-quality code within demanding time constraints.
